Montreal Emergency Room Tracker

See how busy each emergency room is in Montreal. The app is updated every hour to show the current occupancy for each ER. It tracks the median occupancy rates over the week, as well as the occupancy over the past 90 days. The app can predict the occupancy for the next week, based on data that was collected over time.
